Emotion Recognition System With Face Detection

  • START

Emotion is a subjective state of mind.
Emotions can be reactions to internal stimuli (such as thoughts or memories) or events that occur in our environment.
It defines what do we feel about something and facial expressions are the most obvious way to show it.
Facial Expression Recognition (FER) systems are developed to mainly detect
and recognize few core emotions namely: Happiness Sadness, Neutral, Surprise and Anger.
